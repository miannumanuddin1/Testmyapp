Starting Over

Starting over resets the entire program. Youll lose all of the information you already entered in the program. You will still have your information from the dashboard (including the journal, calendar, and worksheets).

Are you sure you want to start over?

Opting Out

Your information is already protected. We remove all identifiers from your information before it is used in group reporting. This process prevents someone from being able to connect you individually with the information. Examples of information that would be not be included in group reports are your name, date of birth, and phone number among other things.

When you opt out, we will not use your de-identified information in our group reports.

Are you sure you want to opt out?

Delete Account

When you delete your profile, you will not be able to see it again, all of your information will be gone (e.g., journal entries). All personally identifiable information will be removed from the system.

If you want to use Mevii again, you will need to start over with a new profile and all new information.

Are you sure you want to delete your account?
